matcha cookies

matcha cookies have a nice tea flavor along with a soft, chewy center, cripsy outer edge, and a nice white chocolate filling and drizzle. it is defintely an unexpecting but delicious treat for everyone (mainly performative males).

ingredients:

  • 2 cups + 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 tbsp matcha powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

for filling:

  • 1 bag of Lindor white chocolate
