chocolate chip cookies

chocolate chip cookies are a classic treat that everyone enjoys! these chocolate chip cookies have a soft, chewy center and a crispy outer edge, perfectly balanced with a hint of salt and sweetness.

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
