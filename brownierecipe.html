brownies

these brownies are super fudgy and an easy to make chocolately treat that everyone will love! they are packed with a deep chocolate flavor, moist, gooey center and a crackly top.

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
